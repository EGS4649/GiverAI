ðŸš€ Getting Started

Giver.ai is an AI-powered social media content generator that helps you create engaging tweets in seconds. Simply tell us your job/niche and your goals, and our AI will generate multiple tweet variations tailored to your audience.

How it works:
  • Enter your profession or niche (e.g., "Digital Marketing Expert")
  • Specify your goal (e.g., "Increase engagement")
  • Choose how many tweets to generate (1-15)
  • Get instant, personalized tweet suggestions

You can try our service once without an account to generate 5 tweets. However, to unlock daily limits, save your tweet history, and access advanced features, you'll need to create a free account.

Free Account Benefits:
  • 15 tweets per day (resets at midnight)
  • 24-hour tweet history
  • Basic customization options
  • Access to your dashboard

Getting started is simple:

  1. Go to TweetGiver or your Dashboard
  2. Fill in your job/niche (be specific for better results)
  3. Describe your goal for today's tweets
  4. Select how many tweets you want (1-5 for free trial, up to 15 with account)
  5. Click "Generate Tweets" and wait a few seconds

Pro tip: The more specific you are about your niche and goals, the better your generated tweets will be!

Good niche descriptions are specific and descriptive. Here are some examples:

Good examples:
  • "Digital Marketing Consultant for SaaS companies"
  • "Personal Fitness Trainer specializing in weight loss"
  • "E-commerce Store Owner selling handmade jewelry"
  • "Tech Startup Founder in the AI space"
Avoid vague descriptions like:
  • "Businessman"
  • "Entrepreneur" (too general)
  • "Content Creator" (what type of content?)

You can update your account information in your Account Settings:

  • Change Password: Enter your current password and choose a new one
  • Update Email: Enter your new email address and confirm
  • Account Info: View your current plan, member since date, and usage stats
Security tip: Use a strong, unique password and keep your email address current for account recovery.

Canceling your subscription is easy:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Find the "Subscription Management" section
  3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Confirm your cancellation

After canceling:

  • You keep access until the end of your current billing cycle
  • Your account reverts to the Free plan automatically
  • No cancellation fees or penalties
  • You can reactivate anytime by upgrading again

Yes, you can permanently delete your account:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Scroll down to the "Delete Account" section
  3. Click "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion (this cannot be undone)
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. All your data, tweet history, and account information will be permanently deleted.

Before deleting, consider:

  • Exporting any tweets you want to keep
  • Canceling your subscription first to avoid billing issues
  • Whether you might want to use the service again in the future

Data handling depends on the action you take:

Subscription Cancellation:
  • Your account data remains intact
  • You revert to Free plan limits
  • Tweet history follows Free plan retention (24 hours)
  • You can reactivate paid features anytime
Account Deletion:
  • All personal data is permanently deleted
  • Tweet history is completely removed
  • Account cannot be recovered
  • Billing history may be retained for legal/accounting purposes

To update your payment method or billing details:

  • Visit your Account Settings
  • Look for the billing or payment section
  • Update your credit card, billing address, or other details

If you're having payment issues:
